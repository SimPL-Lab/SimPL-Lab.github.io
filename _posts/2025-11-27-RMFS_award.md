Sangwon Kim and Hyuntak Lee, together with Professor Soondo Hong, won the Best Paper Award at the 2025 Logistics Paper Competition hosted by the Korean Society of Logistics Science and Technology.

The award-winning paper, titled “Simultaneous Optimization of Module Size and Class-Based Storage Policy in an RMFS Logistics Center,” was recognized for its academic quality and practical relevance in the field of logistics engineering.
