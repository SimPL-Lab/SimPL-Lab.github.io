Hyungkwon Ahn, Donguk Kim, Mingu Kim, and Beomjun Kim won the Grand Prize at the Undergraduate Project Competition of the Korean Society of Industrial and Systems Engineering, under the supervision of Professor Soondo Hong.

Their project was highly recognized for its thoughtful problem definition, systematic analysis, and practical approach to solving an industrial engineering problem. We warmly congratulate the team on this outstanding achievement.
