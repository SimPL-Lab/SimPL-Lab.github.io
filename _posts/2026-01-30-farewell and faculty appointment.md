On January 30, Dr. Bonggwon Kang, a Ph.D. graduate of SimPL lab, has concluded his research activities in Seattle, USA, and a farewell gathering was held with faculty and colleagues on this occasion.
Dr. Bonggwon Kang has been appointed as a faculty member at the Kumoh National Institute of Technology.
We sincerely congratulate Dr. Bonggwon Kang on his appointment and wish him continued success in research and teaching at the new institution.
